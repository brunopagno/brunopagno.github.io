Mat Mate

Mat Mate is a 2D game where you control Dinosir that must avoid obstacles and jump on boxes and walls to collect the flag.

This game was produced in two days in order to test and study some ideas. Feel free to download in any of the following links:

Windows /  Mac /  Jar

If it does not work on your computer please send us a message on the contacts page that we will try to fix it for you. Have fun!
